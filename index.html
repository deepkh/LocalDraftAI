LocalDraft AI

LocalDraft AI is a local-first AI-assisted Markdown editor for writing, editing, grammar correction, and Markdown cleanup.

Open LocalDraft AI App

Privacy and local-first note

LocalDraftAI is local-first. The hosted website may use privacy-friendly Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand page visits. The editor does not send your document content to analytics. Your files stay in your browser/local disk. AI actions only send selected text and the action prompt to the provider you configure.
